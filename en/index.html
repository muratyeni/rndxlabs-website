About RNDX Labs

An independent lab for infrastructure intelligence

Discuss a project →

RNDX Labs is an independent technology lab developing intelligent systems for complex infrastructure environments. We work at the intersection of energy systems, hardware devices, data infrastructure, and monitoring platforms.

Founded by an engineer and systems thinker, RNDX Labs collaborates with municipalities, industrial organizations, energy consultants, and technology partners across Europe.

Our work follows an engineering approach rather than a consulting model. We design systems, prototype quickly, test them in the field, and iterate until they operate reliably in real environments.

Technology

The technological foundation

RNDX Labs combines sensing hardware, data infrastructure, and analytical software to create monitoring systems for complex infrastructure.

Devices capture signals directly from infrastructure environments. Edge computing processes signals locally before transmitting structured data to cloud platforms.

Infrastructure data pipelines transform raw signals into operational intelligence presented through dashboards and analytical tools.

IoT sensing devices — industrial-grade monitoring hardware
Edge computing — local signal processing
Real-time telemetry — continuous infrastructure data streams
Infrastructure data pipelines — reliable data ingestion and processing
AI-assisted analytics — anomaly detection and pattern recognition
Energy monitoring systems — smart-meter integration and grid insight
Cloud dashboards — operational visibility and reporting
Hardware Engineering & Manufacturing

RNDX Labs develops custom monitoring hardware designed for demanding infrastructure environments.

Through close collaboration and co-ownership in an electronics manufacturing facility specialized in SMD PCB assembly, the lab has direct access to professional electronics production.

This enables rapid hardware prototyping, strict quality control, and efficient production of custom sensing devices. The facility supports both prototype production and small to medium series manufacturing, enabling partners to move efficiently from concept to deployable hardware.

Concept → Hardware Design → Rapid Prototyping → Field Testing → Series Production

Research & Innovation

The lab approach

Explore collaboration

RNDX Labs operates as a research and development lab focused on real infrastructure challenges.

Each solution begins with identifying a real operational problem. Concepts are explored through rapid hardware and software prototyping.

Thanks to direct access to electronics manufacturing, prototypes can be produced and tested quickly. Systems are validated through pilot projects and field deployments.

Technologies evolve through continuous iteration and collaboration with infrastructure operators and technical partners. Successful prototypes can be prepared for small-series production and real-world deployment.
