About RNDX Labs

Intelligent systems for complex environments

Discuss a project →

RNDX Labs develops hardware and data systems for monitoring and analysing complex infrastructure. Our technology is deployed in energy systems, industrial installations, and distributed energy environments.

The lab works with municipalities, industry, energy consultants, and technology partners across Europe.

Our approach is engineering-driven. We design systems, build prototypes, test in the field, and iterate until they perform reliably in real infrastructure environments.

Technology

Hardware, sensors, and data platforms

RNDX Labs develops hardware and data systems for energy management, energy monitoring, and infrastructure intelligence.

IoT sensors collect data from energy and infrastructure systems. Edge computing processes signals locally before transmitting data to cloud platforms.

Data pipelines convert raw measurement data into insights delivered through dashboards and analytics platforms.

IoT sensors — hardware for energy and infrastructure monitoring
Edge computing — local data processing
Real-time telemetry — continuous energy and infrastructure data
Data pipelines — data ingestion and processing
AI analytics — anomaly detection
Energy monitoring — smart meter integration
Cloud dashboards — monitoring and reporting
Hardware Engineering & Production

RNDX Labs develops custom hardware for energy and infrastructure systems.

Through a partnership and co-ownership stake in an SMD PCB production facility, the lab has direct access to professional electronics manufacturing.

This enables rapid hardware prototyping and small to mid-volume series production.

Concept → Hardware design → Prototyping → Field testing → Series production
